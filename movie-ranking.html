The Best Movies According To Victor

My Top 3 Movies Of All-Time

Fight Club

Arguably Edward Norton and Brad Pitt at their peak. Super interesting philosophy referenced throughout the film with shades of Buddhist and Stoic elements.

Everything Everywhere All At Once

Incredible visual effects and a super beautiful message behind the film -- that everything is a mess and no one actually knows what the hell is going on. But despite of all that, at least being kind to others is a great start.

A Clockwork Orange

This film was my first experience of Stanley Kubrick's work. It had a pretty powerful impact on me in my university days. The movie's soundtrack had a somehwat disturbing effect on the psyche and was rather subversive by overlaying upbeat music in violent scenes. I remember sitting mouth agape during the film's closing credits with 'Singing in the Rain' bombarding my conscience and humanity.
